About Me

My interest in AI started with seeing how quickly the AEC industry is beginning to shift.

Background

Hi, I’m Marta Bollinger.

I have a degree in civil engineering and am currently pursuing a master’s in business administration and architectural engineering with a focus in construction management. Through my studies, I have become really interested in how the AEC industry is starting to shift with new innovations and technologies.

A lot of what I have learned has been on the more traditional side, so seeing how artificial intelligence is beginning to impact design, decision-making, and construction processes has been especially interesting to me. Understanding how projects come together and how systems can be improved, not just designed, is something I enjoy.

This portfolio highlights my work throughout the semester, including AI news, class assignments, and examples of how AI can be applied in the AEC industry, along with how my perspective on it has developed over the course.

What This Portfolio Reflects

A mix of engineering thinking, industry curiosity, and practical application.

Technical Foundation

My civil engineering background gave me a strong appreciation for structure, systems, accuracy, and the realities of project work.

Industry Awareness

Graduate study has helped me connect traditional AEC processes with newer conversations about AI, automation, and decision support.

Applied Learning

This site is not just about ideas. It shows how AI tools can connect to actual design, estimating, and construction workflows.
