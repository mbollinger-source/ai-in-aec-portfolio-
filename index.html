Why This Matters

AI is not replacing the AEC industry. It is changing how professionals think, work, and make decisions.

What interests me most is not just the technology itself, but what it means for real projects. The AEC industry depends on coordination, judgment, accuracy, and communication. AI has the potential to support all of those areas, which is why understanding it now feels important.
