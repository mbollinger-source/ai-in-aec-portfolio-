Project / Assignment

Reviewing Custom-Built Mini-Agents

An assignment focused on testing AI tools and evaluating what they do well, where they fall short, and whether they are the right tool for the job.

Featured GPT

YouTube Summarizer

Worked well: I like how it organized the summary into sections and hit on the major problems.

Could be improved: The GPT could add just a little more referencing.

Right tool: I do think it was the right tool for the job; although, the YouTube link did not work. I had to submit a transcript.

AEC GPT

Architecture AI

Worked well: The GPT gave great picture inspiration or ideas for designing a space.

Could be improved: However, it did not design a space. It mainly lists concepts or ideas. The cost-optimized version is not truly accurate. It misses other concepts that would be included in the cost. I asked it to design the floorplan, but it did not do it correctly or make the 3D rendering. The GPT just kept listing ideas.

Right tool: I think this is the right tool for inspiration but not the right tool for actually designing.

Project Theme

Reviewing AI mini-agents

Tools Reviewed

YouTube Summarizer + Architecture AI

Key Takeaway

AI tools can be useful for organization and inspiration, but they still need human judgment and oversight.

Overall Reflection

Mini-agents can be helpful, but they are not perfect replacements for human work.

This assignment showed that AI tools can support summaries, ideas, and organization, but they still have limitations when accuracy, design quality, and real project execution matter.
