Togal.AI

AI Estimating Tool

This project explored Togal.AI, a tool designed to automate quantity takeoffs and improve estimating efficiency in construction workflows. The platform uses AI to analyze plan sets and quickly generate takeoff quantities, reducing manual effort and increasing speed.

The tool demonstrates how AI can streamline preconstruction processes and improve accuracy when working with large sets of drawings and data.

Mini-Agent Review

Evaluating AI Tools

This assignment focused on reviewing two AI tools: a YouTube Summarizer and an Architecture AI model. The YouTube summarizer performed well by organizing content clearly and identifying key ideas, although it required transcripts instead of direct video input. The Architecture AI tool was helpful for generating design inspiration but struggled to produce accurate layouts or complete design outputs.

Overall, this project highlighted the strengths and limitations of current AI tools, showing that while they are powerful for ideation and organization, they still require human oversight for accuracy and execution.

Custom Mini-Agent

AI Meeting Assistant

This project involved creating a custom AI agent designed to act as a meeting assistant. The tool organizes notes into structured summaries, identifies key discussion points, and generates action items for teams.

While effective for organization, the agent highlighted challenges in interpreting deadlines and prioritizing tasks, showing both the potential and limitations of AI in real-world applications.
